Speaking & Events

  • STEM Competitions: A Closer Look by STEMconnector (January, 2014) "Competitions continue to emerge as a major outlet for students to engage in STEM exploration. From robotics to cybersecurity to rocket design, many students today have access to experiences that have the potential to expand and enhance their mastery of STEM skills and provide them leadership opportunities and access to career exploration."
  • White House Safety Datapalooza (January, 2014) "Highlighting innovators from the private, nonprofit, and academic sectors who have used freely available government data to build products, services, and apps that advance public safety in creative and powerful ways."
  • Pennsylvania Capitol Rotunda education press conference (November, 2013) Harrisburg, PA. Represented PA Families for Public Cyber Schools. Spoke to the press regarding my experiences as a student attending a public cyber charter school.
  • Pennsylvania Senate Education Committee hearing (October, 2013) Harrisburg, PA. Represented Commonwealth Connections Academy and testified regarding my experiences as a student attending a public cyber charter school.
  • Aerospace Industries Association meet and greet (October, 2013) Washington, DC. Ambassador for the National Center for Women in Information Technology. Presented my Light Sport Aircraft, Search and Rescue with Unmanned Aircraft, and Precision Agriculture with Unmanned Aircraft work on behalf of the CCA Innovators team. 
  • Real World Design Challenge FY14 kick-off event (October, 2013) Washington, DC. Presented my Light Sport Aircraft and Search and Rescue with Unmanned Aircraft work with other team members on behalf of the CCA Innovators. 
  • TechAmerica's “Faith in America’s Future” Presidential Inauguration brunch (January, 2013) Microsoft Research in Washington, DC. Presented my Light Sport Aircraft, Search and Rescue with Unmanned Aircraft, and Precision Agriculture with Unmanned Aircraft work on behalf of the CCA Innovators team.
