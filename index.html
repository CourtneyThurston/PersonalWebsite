Hey there! I’m a high school student, a systems engineer, and an advocate for project-based learning; I'm pretty sure caffeine runs in my veins at this point. Need some research done, or looking for an intern? Feel free to drop me a line here or on LinkedIn! 

Among other projects, I spend most of my time developing airplanes as a competitor in the Real World Design Challenge.  I'm currently the team lead for the CCA Innovators (national rank: 2), where I dabble in research, systems engineering, documentation, and aircraft design. I'm developing sUAS for applications in search & rescue and precision  agriculture, and also working on sensor and scanner packages for the aerospace industry. I'm also currently in the process of co-founding Magikstra, a startup to help connect high school students to mentors.
